Our Mission

At NSW Health for Rural & Regional Communities, our mission is to ensure every individual in regional and remote New South Wales can access timely, quality healthcare, no matter where they live.

We aim to bridge the gap between rural communities and general practitioners by offering fast, simple online consultations through a guided form. By removing the barriers of distance and wait times, we empower people to share their concerns and connect with doctors who care.

Your health matters, wherever you are.
